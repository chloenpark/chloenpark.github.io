Projects

Yummy Food Application

This app was developed by my team in hopes of creating a place for individuals who have an interest in cooking. We analyzed various applications that are used for quick and easy recipes, and through interviews, discovered that users had trouble with these various applications because of its usability difficulties including: lack of specific categories for videos, unclear watch later button, absence of ingredient list and where to find them. After gathering all of this information, my team created the Yummy Application, in hopes of overcoming these problems found in other applications. We used Figma to put our ideas to life, placing different recipes within categories and under specific hashtags as to appeal to the correct audiences. We added a watch later icon that was clearly visible to the user as well as an ingredient list that could be easily accessed. This ingredient list not only showed where you can find the items that were needed but could even be purchased through the application itself. The user is also able to navigate through different sorting methods including Most Popular, Most Viewed, and Most Recent. Through these improvements, we hope the user will not only find the application easy to use, but also experience an enjoyable environment and community.

Yelp Application Improvements

In this project, my team aimed to focus on how we can improve the Yelp application. Through interviews and trials, our team found that the lack of structure and specificity within the reviews was facilitating the flow of misinformation to the user. This could be detrimental to businesses, especially small businesses that depended on their customers and positive reviews in order to gain traction. We found that by altering the reviewing process, the user will be given information that is relevant to their particular desires and wants. The current Yelp rating system presents an overall picture for an independent business that is vague and leaves room for much bias. More specifically, the 5-star rating system allows consumers to leave a rating based on their “overall experience” with the business, and this “overall experience” can be easily influenced by a single interaction between customer and employee. Ultimately, this skews the overall rating of an establishment and does not properly describe the business’s overall performance. Not only this, but the reviews themselves are not always up to date. A business may grow and change over time, and by not allowing there to be an option where the user can filter the most recent reviews in order to get a more accurate view, the business may suffer from past mistakes. By providing a way to filter older reviews from newer ones, the user will be able to have a better understanding of the overall business, and likewise, the business owner can be given the chance to correct their past wrongs.

dizzychlo Sticker Shop

My sticker business was born during the summer of 2020, during the height of covid 19, when people around the world were stuck in their homes with a large amount of time to themselves. I initially started this business with little expectation of actually getting many sales. It was mostly a creative outlet where I could design cute stickers to give to friends and family. I had never sold my own works of art before, so I was very excited to see the business take off after a few months. I had the business from June 2020 - March 2021. Due to getting a job at a coffee shop called Bing Haus around the end of March, I put my online sticker business on hold. With the approval of my boss, I started selling these stickers at Bing Haus which was really special to me. I am opening up my business again at the end of August 2021 and will be selling both stickers and handmade crafts. I used the application Procreate on my Apple iPad and a Cricut machine to create these stickers.
